                Common Vishing Techniques

                🎭 Impersonation

                Pretending to be bank, tech support, government agency, or utility company

                ⚖️ Authority Abuse

                Claiming legal authority, court orders, or threatening arrest/lawsuits

                😰 Fear Tactics

                Creating panic about account closure, fraud, or legal consequences

                ⏰ Artificial Urgency

                "Act now or account will be closed" - preventing rational thinking

                🔒 Trust Building

                Sounding professional, having correct information to build false trust

                🚫 Prevent Verification

                "Don't call back, don't tell anyone, stay on the line" - blocking verification
